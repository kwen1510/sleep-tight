How to talk through it

Keep it simple: Sleep Tight uses the last signals before bed, then turns them into a room routine that gets better over time.

  1. 1
    9:55 p.m.Phone sends 24-hour vitals. Watch records calibrated heart rate for 10 minutes.
  2. 2
    10:05 p.m.Codex pulls the latest data, analyzes sleep quality where possible, and chooses light plus sound.
  3. 3
    BedtimeThe lamp dims and background sound fades to help the user wind down.
  4. 4
    MonthlyCodex summarizes what worked and personalizes the next routine.
