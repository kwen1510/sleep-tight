Personal recipe after 30 days

What Codex would keep

  • Begin after the 22:05 analysis
    Start the wind-down as soon as the device handoff is complete.
  • Use amber light for 30 minutes
    Fade gently all the way to darkness.
  • Choose instrumental or silence
    Instrumental helped on tense days; silence remained best on already-calm days.
  • Avoid brown noise for this person
    It produced worse morning ratings in this synthetic trial.
Confidence: promising, not proven.
Repeat the winning routine for another month before treating it as stable.
