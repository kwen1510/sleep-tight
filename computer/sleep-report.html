Learning rule

Measure whether heart rate and movement begin settling. Never infer relaxation, anxiety or nightmares from heart rate alone.

Tiny nudge rule: at minute 20, if both heart rate and movement have not begun settling, offer one user-approved breathing haptic. No repeated or overnight vibration.
Synthetic wellness demonstration only. Similarity is descriptive, not causal proof. Consumer heart-rate, movement and sleep estimates are not medical measurements.
