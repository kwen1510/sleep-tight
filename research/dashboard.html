02 · Practical protocol

A routine you can use tonight

The watch observes and records. The scheduled environment does most of the work.

MorningGet bright light

Daylight strengthens the contrast between day and the dim evening.

3 h before bedLower melanopic dose

Aim below 10 lux melanopic EDI at eye level where practical.

45 min before bedStart wind-down

Fade warm, low-melanopic light and optionally play familiar low-arousal music.

Lights-outDarkness and silence

Target below 1 lux melanopic EDI. Use masking only for a real noise problem.

During sleepWatch, don’t react

Record movement and cardiac trends. Do not brighten or raise sound from HR alone.

Morning reviewRate before looking

Record perceived quality and alertness, then compare multi-night watch trends.

03 · Smartwatch use

What your watch can contribute

Useful signals are indirect and probabilistic. Combine them over time; never turn one sample into a diagnosis or a stimulus.

Signals and safe uses

Galaxy Watch4 and later can expose these through Samsung APIs on supported models.

Heart rateMeasured from PPGDeviation from personal sleeping baseline; sustained descent or elevationLog trends. Never infer mood or increase sound from a single rise.
IBI / pulse variabilityMeasured intervals, quality-dependentAutonomic recovery trend across clean, comparable nightsUse next morning and over weeks; reject motion-corrupted intervals.

Useful composite markers

These are product research targets, not clinical diagnoses.

Settling probability

Several minutes of lower movement plus a plausible HR descent relative to the same user.

Live · medium confidence
Probable arousal burden

Sustained movement and cardiac change, counted across the night rather than acted on immediately.

Live log · low-medium confidence
Recovery deviation

Sleeping HR above baseline, lower clean-signal HRV, fragmented movement, and poor morning rating.

Morning · trend only
Schedule regularity

Variance in session start, probable sleep onset, and wake time across at least one week.

Weekly · useful
Data confidence

Contact quality, packet completeness, motion artefact, battery, and contradictory channels.

Always · mandatory
